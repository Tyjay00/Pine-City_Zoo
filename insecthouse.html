INSECT HOUSE

Discover the tiny creatures that play a big role in our ecosystem. The Insect House is home to a fascinating collection of beetles, butterflies, ants, and more.

Explore interactive exhibits and learn how insects help pollinate plants, recycle nutrients, and even defend themselves in clever ways.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Insect House is located near the Butterfly Garden, just east of the central fountain.
