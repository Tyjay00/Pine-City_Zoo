LOST FOREST

Step into the mysterious Lost Forest, where nature thrives in its wildest form. Wander along winding paths surrounded by towering trees, lush ferns, and hidden wildlife.

This immersive experience lets you explore what ancient forests might have looked like, complete with the sounds of birds, rustling leaves, and trickling streams.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest is located on the southern edge of the park, beyond the Gorilla Enclosure.
